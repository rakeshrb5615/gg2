# Legacy Performance Issues
Until version 0.94, the app suffered from major performance limitations. Despite significant debugging efforts, the Android canvas and animation APIs couldn't handle the volume of real-time drawing and computation required. 
Even at reduced frame rates (e.g., 24‚ÄØFPS), the rendering pipeline remained too slow, especially in the main activity. This slow performance was a major drawback that led many users to uninstall the app shortly after launching it.
